# LinkedIn Post Template

*Refer to TONE-AND-VOICE-GUIDE.md for voice principles.*

---

## Template

```
[HOOK: 2 lines max. Must compel "see more" click.]
[Choose a hook pattern: surprising finding / contrarian take / builder announcement / landscape clarity]

[CONTEXT: 2-3 sentences. Set up the problem or landscape.]

[INSIGHT: The core of the post. 3-5 sentences. What you built, found, or concluded.]
[Include specific numbers, protocol names, real scenarios.]

[EVIDENCE: Show don't tell. Reference your code, demo, diagram, or data.]
[Link to GitHub, attach screenshot/GIF, or describe a specific result.]

[TAKEAWAY: 1-2 sentences. The "so what" for the reader.]

[QUESTION: End with a specific, answerable question to drive comments.]

---
#AgenticPayments #MCP #AI #Payments #Fintech #AgentDevelopment
```

---

## Hook Examples

**Surprising finding:**
"My AI agent found a remittance route 80% cheaper than SWIFT. Here's what happened."

**Contrarian take:**
"Everyone's talking about agentic payments. Almost nobody is talking about the hardest problem: reconciliation."

**Builder announcement:**
"I just shipped an MCP server with 6 payment tools. Any AI agent can now check balances, initiate transfers, and flag fraud."

**Landscape clarity:**
"3 protocols. 3 different trust models. 1 problem: how do AI agents pay for things securely? Here's the breakdown."

---

## Checklist Before Posting

- [ ] Hook is compelling in 2 lines (would YOU click "see more"?)
- [ ] Includes at least one specific number, protocol, or real example
- [ ] Demonstrates builder energy (you made/tested/discovered something)
- [ ] Shows product sense (who needs this, what's the commercial implication)
- [ ] Includes a visual (diagram, GIF, screenshot, architecture)
- [ ] Ends with a specific question
- [ ] No JPMorgan references or proprietary information
- [ ] Tagged relevant companies/people
- [ ] 150-300 words
- [ ] Hashtags included
